About Us

Why Choose Us?

St. Augustine Medical centre is a faith based healthcare facility which was set up in 2020 and is designed to offer holistic, competitive and affordable medical services and to fill a gap in healthcare delivery in Taita Taveta and its environs. We realized that, there is part of the population of people who are looking for a place where all their medical needs can be met in a unique way and with a Christian touch. Due to that longing need, many have opted to cross over to the neighboring country (Tanzania) incurring a lot of cost to quench their need. The desire to meet their needs at an affordable cost resulted in the establishment of St. Augustine Medical Centre.

We endeavor to glorify God in everything we do. We understand that human beings have special physical, social, emotional and spiritual needs and our vast range of services will ensure that all your needs are met under one roof. We believe that by doing so, God is glorified when we meet the needs of His people. At St. Augustine Medical Centre, we treat every client diligently because we believe that by doing so, our clients will be satisfied and God is glorified.

St. Augustine Medical Centre offers general and preventative health care for all ages in Taita Taveta and the surrounding communities. The clinic utilizes trained staff, quality equipment and a well-coordinated referral system in order to maximize the care for each patient.



Mission :

Our mission is to promote the health and well-being of the local population by providing accessible, affordable and high-quality medical care for people of all ages. St. Augustine Medical Centre is committed to providing services that will exceed the expectations of our clients and in so doing, glorify God.



Vision :

Our vision is to transform healthcare in Taita Taveta and its environs with the love of Christ, by providing quality, affordable, holistic and compassionate care to the community and beyond.
